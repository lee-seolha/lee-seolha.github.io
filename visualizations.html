Trends Graph

Visualization created using The Prelude, 1805. More discussion here.

The line graph shows the relative frequencies of words grouped into each topic of sight, hearing, and nature to reveal the relationships between nature and the senses of sight and hearing in William Wordsworth's Prelude (I want to add touch, hand). It shows that although not drastically distinct, the words regarding sight generally appear more frequently than the other groups of words.

Visualization of the Wordsworth Collection (Corpus 2) using TermsBerry.
